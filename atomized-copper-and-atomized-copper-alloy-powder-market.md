<p><p>The Atomized Copper and Atomized Copper Alloy Powder market is categorized into Water Atomized and Air Atomized types. Water atomization involves the rapid cooling of molten metal using water jets, resulting in finer and more uniform powder particles, often ideal for high-density applications. Air atomization employs high-pressure air to break up molten metal, producing larger particles with varied shapes, suitable for less demanding applications. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, particularly in additive manufacturing and powder metallurgy.</p></p>
