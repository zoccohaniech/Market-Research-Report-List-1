<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Sorting Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Sorting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic plastic sorting machine market primarily includes two types: chute-type and belt-type sorting machines. Chute-type sorting machines utilize gravity to guide plastics down a sloped surface, effectively separating materials by weight and size. In contrast, belt-type sorting machines use conveyor belts to transport plastic materials, employing sensors and cameras for precise sorting based on specific characteristics. Both types enhance recycling efficiency, reduce contamination, and support sustainability efforts in waste management by improving the recovery of recyclable plastics.</p></p>
