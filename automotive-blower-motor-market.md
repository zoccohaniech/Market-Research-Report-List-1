<p><ul><li>Brushed Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive blower motor market is primarily divided into two types: brushed motors and brushless motors. Brushed motors use carbon brushes to transmit electricity to the rotor, resulting in a simple and cost-effective design, ideal for lower-end vehicles. In contrast, brushless motors eliminate brushes, offering higher efficiency, reduced wear, and longer lifespan. They are increasingly favored in modern vehicles for their performance and quieter operation. The choice between these motor types affects vehicle energy efficiency and overall performance.</p></p>
