<p><p>Form Fill Seal (FFS) machines play a crucial role in packaging, categorized mainly into vertical and horizontal markets. Vertical Form Fill Seal machines operate by creating bags from a roll of film, filling them with products, and sealing them in an upright position, ideal for granular items like rice or sugar. In contrast, Horizontal Form Fill Seal machines function by forming pouches from film sheets, filling them while lying flat, suitable for products like snacks or liquids. Each type addresses specific product and packaging needs efficiently.</p></p>
