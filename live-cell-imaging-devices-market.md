<p><p>Live cell imaging devices encompass various microscopy techniques used to observe living cells in real time. Fluorescent microscopy utilizes fluorescence to visualize specific cell components, allowing for detailed studies of cellular processes. Phase contrast microscopy enhances the contrast of transparent specimens, making it easier to observe live cells without staining. Quantitative phase contrast microscopy provides quantitative data on cell morphology and refractive index variations. Other methods in this market include brightfield and confocal microscopy, which each offer unique advantages for live cell studies.</p></p>
