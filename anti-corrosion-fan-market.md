<p><p>The anti-corrosion fan market is categorized into three main types based on pressure: low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure fans. Low-pressure anti-corrosion fans are designed for applications requiring minimal resistance, suitable for ventilating large spaces. Medium-pressure fans balance airflow and resistance, making them ideal for industrial setups with moderate corrosion risks. High-pressure anti-corrosion fans, on the other hand, are engineered for high resistance environments, delivering powerful airflow necessary for demanding processes in harsh conditions, ensuring equipment longevity and efficiency.</p></p>
