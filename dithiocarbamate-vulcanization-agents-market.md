<p><p>Dithiocarbamate vulcanization agents are essential accelerators used in rubber processing. Key types include ZBEC, ZDBC, ZDEC, ZDMC, NOBS (MBS), ZBED, and SBDC. ZBEC (Zinc bis(dibutyldithiocarbamate)) and ZDBC (Zinc dibutyldithiocarbamate) enhance curing speed and improve thermal stability. ZDEC (Zinc diethyl dithiocarbamate) and ZDMC (Zinc dimethyl dithiocarbamate) provide effective crosslinking. NOBS (N-oxydiethyldithiocarbamate) offers low toxicity, while ZBED (Zinc benzylidine dithiocarbamate) and SBDC (Sodium benzenesulfonic dithiocarbamate) also contribute to enhanced properties, ensuring robust performance in rubber products.</p></p>
