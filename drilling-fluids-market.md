<p><ul><li>Oil-based Fluids (OBF)</li><li>Water-based Fluids (WBF)</li><li>Synthetic-based Fluids (SBF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling fluids market comprises three main types: Oil-Based Fluids (OBF), Water-Based Fluids (WBF), and Synthetic-Based Fluids (SBF). OBFs, composed of mineral oils or synthetic oils, offer excellent lubrication and stability at high temperatures, making them suitable for challenging drilling environments. WBFs, primarily composed of water and various additives, are eco-friendly and cost-effective, ideal for many drilling applications. SBFs combine advantages of both OBFs and WBFs, providing lower toxicity and enhanced performance, thus appealing for specialized drilling projects.</p></p>
