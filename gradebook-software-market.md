<p><p>Gradebook software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based gradebook solutions are hosted online, allowing users to access and manage data from any device with an internet connection, promoting collaboration and ease of updates. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed locally on school servers, offering greater control over data but requiring more IT resources for maintenance and security. Each type serves different institutional needs based on infrastructure, budget, and user preferences.</p></p>
