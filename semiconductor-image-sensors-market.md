<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor image sensor market primarily consists of two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their excellent image quality and low noise, making them popular in high-end applications like professional cameras and scientific imaging. Conversely, CMOS sensors offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for consumer electronics like smartphones and webcams. The choice between the two depends on the specific application requirements and performance preferences.</p></p>
