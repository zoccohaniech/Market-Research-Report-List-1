<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The on-site hydrogen generator market primarily features two types of technologies: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid electrolyte and are known for their robustness and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane and offer high efficiency and rapid response times, making them ideal for dynamic applications and smaller installations. Both types play crucial roles in the growing hydrogen economy by providing clean hydrogen production.</p></p>
