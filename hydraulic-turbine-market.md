<p><strong>The Hydraulic Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Hydraulic Turbine</li><li>Reaction Hydraulic Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic turbine market consists of two main types: impulse and reaction hydraulic turbines. Impulse turbines utilize the kinetic energy of water jets to drive the turbine blades, making them suitable for high-head applications. Reaction turbines, on the other hand, generate power through the pressure change of water, allowing for efficient performance in varying water levels. Both types are essential for harnessing hydroelectric power, catering to diverse energy needs and geographical conditions in renewable energy generation.</p></p>
