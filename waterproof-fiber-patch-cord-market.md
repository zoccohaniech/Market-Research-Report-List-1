<p><p>The waterproof fiber patch cord market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode fiber optical patch cords. Single-mode fiber patch cords offer a narrow core that allows for high-speed data transmission over long distances, making them ideal for telecommunication and internet services. In contrast, multimode fiber patch cords feature a larger core, enabling multiple light modes to transmit data over shorter distances, which is suitable for local area networks and data centers. Both types are designed for durability and resistance to moisture.</p></p>
