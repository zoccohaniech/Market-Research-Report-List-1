<p><ul><li>Gas-flow Proportional Counter</li><li>Sealed Tubes Proportional Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Proportional counters are devices used to detect and measure ionizing radiation. The gas-flow proportional counter operates by using a continuous flow of gas that amplifies the ionization signals from radiation. Sealed tube proportional counters, on the other hand, contain a fixed amount of gas within a sealed environment, providing higher stability and sensitivity. Both types are utilized in various applications, including medical diagnostics, nuclear industry, and environmental monitoring, due to their ability to accurately count and measure radiation levels.</p></p>
