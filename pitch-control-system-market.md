<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pitch Control System</li><li>Electric Pitch Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pitch control system market is primarily divided into hydraulic and electric pitch control systems. Hydraulic pitch control systems utilize fluid pressure to adjust the angle of wind turbine blades, offering high reliability and robust performance, particularly in harsh environments. In contrast, electric pitch control systems employ electric motors and sensors for precise blade adjustments, providing faster response times and enhanced efficiency. Both system types are critical for optimizing energy output and ensuring the safe operation of wind turbines in varying wind conditions.</p></p>
