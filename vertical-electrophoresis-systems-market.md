<p><p>Vertical electrophoresis systems are laboratory equipment designed for the separation of biomolecules based on size and charge, primarily used for proteins and nucleic acids. These systems utilize a gel medium, typically polyacrylamide or agarose, where samples are loaded into wells and subjected to an electric field that promotes the movement of charged particles. The vertical orientation of these systems allows for enhanced resolution and the ability to run multiple samples simultaneously, making them a preferred choice in molecular biology and biochemistry research.</p><p>The Vertical Electrophoresis Systems Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period.
