<p><ul><li>Pacemakers</li><li>Defibrillators</li><li>Ablation Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arrhythmia management devices market comprises three main types: pacemakers, defibrillators, and ablation devices. Pacemakers regulate heart rhythms by delivering electrical impulses, ensuring a steady heartbeat. Defibrillators, such as Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), treat life-threatening arrhythmias by delivering shocks to restore normal heart function. Ablation devices target and destroy abnormal heart tissue causing irregular rhythms, utilizing technologies like radiofrequency or cryoablation. These devices play crucial roles in treating arrhythmias, enhancing patient outcomes, and improving quality of life.</p></p>
