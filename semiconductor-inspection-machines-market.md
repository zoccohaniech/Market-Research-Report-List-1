<p><p>The semiconductor inspection machines market comprises two main types: defect inspection equipment and metrology equipment. Defect inspection equipment identifies and analyzes defects in wafers and chips during the manufacturing process, ensuring quality control and reducing yield losses. Metrology equipment, on the other hand, measures various physical parameters of semiconductor devices, such as dimensions and layer thickness, to ensure that they meet specified tolerances and specifications. Both types are crucial for maintaining efficiency, reliability, and performance in semiconductor production.</p></p>
