<p><p>The spectral sensing filters market includes several types, notably dichroic filters, band pass filters, and others. Dichroic filters selectively reflect certain wavelengths while transmitting others, making them ideal for applications in imaging and lighting. Band pass filters allow only a specific range of wavelengths to pass through, enhancing signal quality in various sensing applications. Other filters may include notch filters or long and short pass filters, each serving unique roles in separating or attenuating specific spectral ranges for improved analysis and measurement.</p></p>
