<p><p>The high-temperature sterilization market encompasses various methods, primarily steam sterilization, dry sterilization, and other techniques. Steam sterilization utilizes saturated steam under pressure to eliminate microorganisms, making it highly effective for heat-resistant instruments. Dry sterilization employs high temperatures without moisture, ideal for heat-sensitive materials that could be damaged by steam. Other methods may include chemical sterilization or radiation, offering alternative solutions based on specific needs. Each approach plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and sterility of medical and laboratory equipment.</p></p>
