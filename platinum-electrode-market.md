<p><ul><li>Counter Electrode</li><li>Single Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The platinum electrode market is primarily divided into two types: counter electrodes and single electrodes. Counter electrodes are utilized in electrochemical cells to complete the circuit, facilitating reactions alongside the working electrode. They are essential for balancing reactions and improving efficiency. In contrast, single electrodes are standalone units used in various applications, including sensors and electroanalysis. Both types leverage the properties of platinum, such as conductivity and corrosion resistance, making them vital in research and industrial processes.</p></p>
