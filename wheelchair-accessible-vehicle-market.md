<p><strong>The Wheelchair Accessible Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Entry</li><li>Rear Entry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wheelchair Accessible Vehicle (WAV) market primarily features two types: side entry and rear entry vehicles. Side entry models allow wheelchair users to enter from the side, making them suitable for urban environments with limited parking space. They typically have a fold-out ramp and offer easy access to the driver’s seat and passenger area. Rear entry vehicles, conversely, provide access from the back, accommodating larger models and allowing for more passengers. Both options enhance mobility and independence for users.</p></p>
