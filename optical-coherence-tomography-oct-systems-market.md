<p><strong>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TD-OCT</li><li>SD-OCT</li><li>SS-OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) systems are categorized into three main types: Time Domain OCT (TD-OCT), Spectral Domain OCT (SD-OCT), and Swept Source OCT (SS-OCT). TD-OCT uses a reference arm to measure light delay, providing lower resolution images. SD-OCT employs a spectrometer for higher resolution and faster imaging, making it more popular in clinical settings. SS-OCT utilizes a tunable laser to achieve even faster imaging speeds and greater penetration depth, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in ophthalmology and other medical fields.</p></p>
