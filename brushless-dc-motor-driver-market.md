<p><ul><li>Surface Magnetic Pole</li><li>Embedded Magnetic Pole</li><li>Annular Magnetic Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brushless DC Motor Driver market comprises three main types based on magnetic pole design: Surface Magnetic Pole, Embedded Magnetic Pole, and Annular Magnetic Pole. Surface Magnetic Pole motors feature magnets mounted on the rotor's surface, offering high efficiency and simplicity. Embedded Magnetic Pole designs have magnets embedded within the rotor, providing compactness and improved torque characteristics. Annular Magnetic Pole motors utilize a ring-like arrangement of magnets, enhancing performance and thermal management. Each type serves distinct applications based on efficiency, size, and performance requirements.</p></p>
