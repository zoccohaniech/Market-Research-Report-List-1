<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotatory evaporators market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and functionality. Horizontal rotatory evaporators use a horizontal positioning of the evaporation flask, allowing for efficient heat transfer and uniform vaporization. They are often favored for large-scale operations. In contrast, vertical rotatory evaporators have an upright configuration, optimizing space and facilitating easier collection of condensed solvents. Both types are essential in labs for solvent evaporation, distillation, and concentration processes, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
