<p><ul><li>Semi-automated</li><li>Fully automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Public Access Defibrillator (PAD) market consists of two main types: semi-automated and fully automated defibrillators. Semi-automated devices require the operator to deliver the shock after the device analyzes the heart rhythm, offering guidance through voice prompts. In contrast, fully automated defibrillators take complete control of the process, automatically delivering shocks as needed without operator intervention. Both types aim to increase survival rates during cardiac emergencies by providing accessible life-saving technology to the public.</p></p>
