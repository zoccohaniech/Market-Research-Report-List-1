<p><p>Vertical tripod turnstiles are classified into two main market types: automatic and mechanical. Automatic turnstiles utilize advanced technology, allowing for seamless access control via RFID, biometrics, or proximity cards. These systems enhance security and streamline entry processes. In contrast, mechanical turnstiles operate on a simpler, manual mechanism, requiring users to physically push the arm to gain entry. While mechanical models are often more cost-effective, automatic turnstiles offer greater convenience and integration with modern security systems, making them popular in high-traffic areas.</p></p>
