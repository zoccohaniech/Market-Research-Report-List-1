<p><strong>The Titanium Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shell and Tube</li><li>Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium heat exchanger market primarily features two types: shell and tube, and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of multiple tubes enclosed within a shell, allowing efficient heat transfer between two fluids. They are suitable for high-pressure applications. Plate heat exchangers, on the other hand, consist of thin plates arranged to create channels for fluid flow, providing a compact design and high thermal efficiency. Both types are favored for their resistance to corrosion and heat conductivity in demanding applications.</p></p>
