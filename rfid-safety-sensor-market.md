<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID safety sensor market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact RFID sensors require physical interaction with the tagged object, often used for applications where precise positioning and security are paramount. In contrast, non-contact RFID sensors enable identification and tracking without direct contact, making them ideal for scenarios where quick scanning or bulk tracking is needed, such as inventory management. Both types enhance safety and efficiency across various industries, ensuring streamlined operations.</p></p>
