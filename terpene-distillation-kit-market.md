<p><p>The terpene distillation kit market features various methods for extracting terpenes, primarily including microwave distillation and steam distillation. Microwave distillation utilizes microwave energy to quickly heat plant materials, resulting in efficient terpene extraction with minimal thermal degradation. In contrast, steam distillation involves passing steam through plant materials, causing the volatile terpenes to evaporate and be collected. Both methods cater to different production needs, with microwave distillation offering faster results and steam distillation providing a traditional, well-established extraction technique.</p></p>
