<p><p>The solvent recycling system market encompasses two primary types: batch processing and continuous processing. Batch processing involves treating a specific amount of solvent in discrete batches, allowing for flexibility and easier management of varying waste types. In contrast, continuous processing operates on a continual flow basis, ensuring a constant output of recycled solvent, which enhances efficiency and reduces downtime. Both methods serve to minimize solvent waste and promote sustainability, catering to diverse industrial needs and operational scales.</p></p>
