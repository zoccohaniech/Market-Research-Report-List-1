<p><p>The sleeve applicator market encompasses two main types: linear applicators and rotary applicators. In contrast, rotary applicators utilize a rotating mechanism that allows for more versatile and efficient sleeve application on various container shapes. Both types cater to different production needs, with linear applicators focusing on simplicity and speed, while rotary applicators offer flexibility and adaptability in diverse packaging environments. Overall, these technologies enhance operational efficiency and product presentation.</p></p>
