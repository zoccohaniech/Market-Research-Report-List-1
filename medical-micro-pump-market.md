<p><p>The medical micro pump market comprises two primary types: electric and magnetic drive pumps. Electric pumps utilize motors to create fluid movement, offering precise control and adaptability for various medical applications, such as drug delivery and infusion therapy. In contrast, magnetic drive pumps leverage magnetic fields to operate without direct contact with the fluid, minimizing contamination risk and enhancing safety. Both types serve crucial roles in healthcare, ensuring accurate dosing and efficient fluid management in diverse medical settings.</p></p>
