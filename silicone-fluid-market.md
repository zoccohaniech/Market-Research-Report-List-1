<p><p>The silicone fluid market consists of two main types: straight silicone fluid and modified silicone fluid. Straight silicone fluids are low-viscosity, stable compounds used primarily for lubrication, insulation, and coatings due to their excellent thermal stability and low surface tension. Modified silicone fluids, however, incorporate additives or structural changes, enhancing properties for specific applications like emulsifiers, surfactants, and anti-foaming agents. These distinct types cater to diverse industrial needs, including automotive, personal care, and electronics sectors.</p></p>
