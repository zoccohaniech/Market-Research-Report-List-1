<p><p>Hybrid system market types include Start-Stop, Regenerative Braking, and EV Drive. Start-Stop systems automatically shut off the engine when the vehicle is idle to save fuel and reduce emissions, then restart it when needed. Regenerative Braking captures energy normally lost during braking, converting it into electricity for storage in the battery. EV Drive refers to vehicles that use electric power for propulsion, either solely or alongside a conventional engine, enhancing efficiency and lowering environmental impact.</p></p>
