<p><p>The luxury bathtubs market is divided into two main types: embedded bathtubs and independent bathtubs. Embedded bathtubs are built into the bathroom structure, often providing a seamless, integrated look that enhances the overall aesthetic. They are typically designed for a more permanent installation. In contrast, independent bathtubs, also known as freestanding bathtubs, stand alone and can be positioned anywhere in the bathroom. These offer flexibility in design and can be a striking focal point, reflecting personal style and luxury.</p></p>
