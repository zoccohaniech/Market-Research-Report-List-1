<p><p>The Under Display Fingerprint Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Optical Fingerprint Modules and Ultrasonic Fingerprint Modules. Optical sensors use light to capture fingerprint patterns through the display, offering higher image quality and fast response times. In contrast, Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to create a three-dimensional image of the fingerprint, providing enhanced security and performance in varied conditions. Both technologies aim to improve user experience and device security in smartphones and other electronic devices.</p></p>
