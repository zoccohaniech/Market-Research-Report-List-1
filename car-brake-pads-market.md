<p><p>The car brake pads market includes various types tailored for specific performance and durability needs. Non-asbestos organic (NAO) brake pads are made from natural materials, offering quieter operation. Low metallic NAO brake pads contain small amounts of metal for better heat conduction and performance. Semi-metallic brake pads, composed of metal fibers, provide excellent stopping power and durability but may produce more noise. Ceramic brake pads are made from ceramic compounds, delivering low dust production, durability, and quieter performance, ideal for everyday driving.</p></p>
