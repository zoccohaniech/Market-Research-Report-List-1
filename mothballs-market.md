<p><p>The mothballs market is segmented into two primary types: synthesis mothballs and natural mothballs. Synthesis mothballs are chemically produced, often containing substances like naphthalene or Paradichlorobenzene, which are effective in repelling moths and pests. On the other hand, natural mothballs are made from organic materials, such as cedar or essential oils, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. Both types cater to diverse market demands, balancing efficacy in pest control with consumer preferences for safety and sustainability.</p></p>
