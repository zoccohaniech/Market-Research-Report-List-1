<p><strong>The Polystyrene Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Polystyrene Film Capacitors</li><li>Radial Polystyrene Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polystyrene film capacitors market is generally categorized into two main types: axial and radial polystyrene film capacitors. Axial polystyrene film capacitors feature leads at both ends, making them suitable for through-hole applications where space efficiency is vital. In contrast, radial polystyrene film capacitors have their leads extending from the same side, which is ideal for compact circuit board layouts. Both types are valued for their stability, low dielectric loss, and excellent performance in high-frequency applications.</p></p>
