<p><p>The exterior wall insulation market is categorized into several types: Rock Wool, Glass Wool, PU (Polyurethane), and Others. Rock Wool is known for its fire resistance and soundproofing capabilities, making it ideal for commercial buildings. Glass Wool is lightweight and offers good thermal insulation, often used in residential applications. Polyurethane insulation is renowned for its high thermal efficiency and moisture resistance, suitable for modern constructions. Other materials may include EPS (expanded polystyrene) and cellulose, providing varied options based on specific insulation needs.</p></p>
