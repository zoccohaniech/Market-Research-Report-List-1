<p><p>The linear hydraulic actuator market consists of several types, including single-acting, double-acting, double-rod, rodless, and others. Single-acting actuators utilize hydraulic pressure on one side of the piston, extending in one direction. Double-acting actuators apply pressure on both sides, allowing motion in both directions. Double-rod actuators feature rods on each side, providing balanced force. Rodless actuators eliminate external rods, utilizing a magnetic sliding mechanism for movement. Each type caters to specific applications, balancing design efficiency and space constraints.</p></p>
